In loving memory of Omar Al-Khalidi. One thing that is universally agreed to benefit the dead is to pray for them, appealing to God to forgive them and bestow His mercy on them. The evidence for this is the Qur’anic verse that says: “Those who come after them say: ‘Our Lord! Forgive us our sins, as well as those of our brethren who preceded us in faith, and let not our hearts entertain any unworthy thoughts or feelings against any believers. Our Lord! You are truly compassionate, merciful.” (59: 10)
